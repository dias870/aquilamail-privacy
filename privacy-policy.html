Privacy Policy – SMS to Email Forwarder

Last updated: 2025-11-16

This application (“App”) is developed by Sinfobox (“we”, “us”, “our”). The App allows you to automatically forward incoming SMS messages from your Android device to one or more email addresses, with optional filters and rules.

1. Data we access

The App may access the following data on your device:

  • SMS messages: Used to read incoming SMS in order to forward them to the email addresses you have configured.
  • Contacts (optional): If you grant the Contacts permission, the App may read contact names and phone numbers locally on your device to:
    • display the sender name associated with a phone number; and/or
    • help you select contacts when configuring rules or recipients.
  • Network access (Internet): Used only to send emails using the SMTP server settings you provide (for example: your own email provider or SMTP account).

2. How data is used

  • SMS content is used only to evaluate your filters/rules and forward the message to the email addresses you have configured.
  • Contact data is used only on your device to resolve contact names or help you select recipients.
  • We do not upload your SMS or contacts to our own servers.
  • The App does not collect analytics, advertising identifiers, or tracking information.

3. Data storage and transfer

  • We do not operate any backend server for this App. All processing happens locally on your device.
  • Emails are sent directly from your device to the email server (SMTP) you have configured. Any SMS content or email addresses in the forwarded messages are processed by your email provider according to their own privacy policy.

4. Third parties

  • We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third-party advertisers or analytics services.
  • Your email provider and mobile operator may process SMS and emails according to their own terms and privacy policies.

5. Permissions

The App may request the following permissions:

  • RECEIVE_SMS / READ_SMS – to detect and read incoming SMS in order to apply your rules and forward them to email.
  • READ_CONTACTS (optional) – to display contact names and help you choose recipients. The data stays on the device.
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS – to show status notifications (for example, errors sending emails or rule matches).
  • INTERNET – to send emails via the SMTP server you configure.

6. Children

This App is not directed to children under 13/16 (depending on your jurisdiction), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated “Last updated” date.
